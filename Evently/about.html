Service Above Self

Rotary Club Dharmapuri brings together local leaders and volunteers to deliver sustainable projects in health, education and community development. Our membership is focused on measurable impact and inclusive programs that uplift vulnerable groups across the district.

  • Health camps & free medical screenings
  • Education support & scholarships
  • Community infrastructure and sanitation

Our Mission

To identify tangible community needs and deliver evidence-based projects that improve health outcomes, strengthen education, and empower local leadership. We partner with hospitals, schools and municipal bodies to ensure long-term sustainability.
