Main Venue

Dharmapuri District Sports Stadium

District Sports Stadium, Dharmapuri, Tamil Nadu, India

The Elite Marathon 2025 will be held at the Dharmapuri District Sports Stadium—a spacious open-air venue equipped to handle marathon logistics, large crowds, and event coordination efficiently. The stadium serves as the starting point, running corridor hub, and celebration zone.
